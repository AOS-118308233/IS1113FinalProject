Interests

I am a passionate music fan and I have been for many years, my tastes have differed hugely through the years. Some of my current favourite artists include Tom Odell, George Ezra, Hozier, Bastille London Grammar, Arctic Monkeys and the xx. I have been lucky enough to have seen both Tom Odell and George Ezra in concert, and was introduced to George's music as he was the support act for Tom Odell. These two musicians are my biggest inspiration musically and because of their music, I was inspired to learn how to play the guitar, something I had longed to do for many years. I play the guitar occasionally in my limited free time.

I am also very passionate about Formula 1 and I have been for more than 10 years. I began to watch F1 with my dad on Sunday evenings, quickly becoming an avid fan. As I got older, I began to look into the sport more from a technological perspective. I have always been mind blown by these cars, they are the pinnacle of motor sport technology.
